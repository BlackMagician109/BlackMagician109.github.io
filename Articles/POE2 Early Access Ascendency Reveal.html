path of exile 2 early access ascendency reveal


4 December 2024


Path of Exile 2 just announced all the skills available during ascension for all the classes available at the start of early access coming December 6, 2024


What is Ascension


Ascension unlocks powerful abilities for your characters and are unique to the class you choose. You acquire these points by completing trials of ascension. While the release version of the game will 3 ascendency option for each class, the early trial will have 2 options per class.
